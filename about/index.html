About Us

Who We Are

Bree Holdings is a visionary parent company committed to nurturing and growing innovative ventures across diverse industries. Rooted in excellence and driven by purpose, we aim to shape a future where business and impact align to uplift communities and redefine possibilities.

Our Vision

To become a leading multi-venture brand in Africa known for excellence, integrity, and innovation — influencing industries and empowering lives.

Our Mission

To incubate, invest in, and scale transformative ideas by leveraging technology, creativity, and leadership that inspires sustainable growth and community empowerment.

Our Story

Bree Holdings was founded out of a simple yet bold dream — to build a parent company that would unify purpose-driven ventures under one roof. What began as a passion project in 2021 quickly evolved into a dynamic group of brands across sectors. From humble beginnings to launching disruptive businesses, Bree Holdings continues to write a story of impact, innovation, and transformation — one venture at a time.

Our Values

Innovation

We champion bold thinking and embrace forward-looking ideas that drive transformation.

Integrity

Honesty, transparency, and ethical conduct guide every decision we make.

Excellence

We strive for distinction and deliver quality in everything we do.

Community

We are dedicated to making a positive impact in the communities we serve.

Leadership

We lead by example and empower others to rise and thrive.
